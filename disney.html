About Disney

Disney has been creating magic for over 90 years with iconic films, characters, and stories. Today, it encompasses various entertainment properties, including theme parks, studios, and streaming services, all known for their timeless characters and family-oriented content.

From its humble beginnings in animation to becoming a global entertainment giant, Disney has shaped the world of entertainment through films like *Snow White*, *The Lion King*, and the Marvel Cinematic Universe. Disney continues to bring joy, hope, and inspiration to millions worldwide.

Meet the Characters

Disney is home to a wide range of characters that have captured the hearts of audiences for decades. From the charming Mickey Mouse to the adventurous Elsa, explore these beloved personalities!

Mickey Mouse

Disney's most beloved character, Mickey is known for his optimism, adventurous spirit, and his iconic red shorts.

Donald Duck

Known for his fiery temper and distinct voice, Donald brings humor and heart to Disney stories.

Goofy

Clumsy but lovable, Goofy has been entertaining us with his goofy antics and positive attitude.

Elsa

The Snow Queen from *Frozen*, Elsa's journey from fear to empowerment has resonated with fans around the world.
